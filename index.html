The Beginning...

First things first....

You're classmates are gone, you were hiding in the bathroom after your principle, Dr. Angelo, called in a lock down. You leave the bathroom, curious as of why everything is silent. No footsteps or anything. As you look out the door to check your surroundings, you swiftly make it back to your class. To your surprise, there's no one in there. Only puddles of blood and footprints. You hear a knock on the window and a slight groan, creating an interest to check it out. It was your friend, Jessica with her mouth bloody and her skin paler than a blank sheet of paper. She pounces at the window, staring directly at you. Scared, you drew the blinds and went to the back of the classroom. You know you can't stay in here long, there isn't any food or medical supplies.

Choices

You can stay in the room for the night.

or

Leave the classroom and collect as much of resources as you can.
